The Pioneering Research & Innovation Award was instituted in 2025 by the Class of 1965, the founding batch of IIT Kanpur, to recognise original and high-impact research at the Institute. It now forms part of IIT Kanpur's permanent framework of academic recognition, and its continuation depends on the support of alumni across all batches.

How the endowment works

Following the standard Institute norm, 4% of the endowment's interest is utilised each year. Excess interest is reinvested to cover inflation and ensure future growth — keeping the award perpetual.

Tax benefits

The Vision Behind the Award

A gift from one generation of pioneers to the next.

In March 2025, the Class of 1965 — the first graduating batch of IIT Kanpur — marked its Diamond Jubilee Reunion at the Institute. On this occasion, the batch decided to institute the Pioneering Research & Innovation Award.

More than an award, it is a gift from one generation of pioneers to the next — to ensure that the bold thinking, exceptional talent and culture of innovation that shaped IIT Kanpur continues to inspire for generations to come.

The Impact Behind the Award

Meet the awardees.

Student Awardee · 2025

Vikas Vats

PhD · IIT Kanpur
Computational & Medical Imaging

During my doctoral research, I focused on developing imaging algorithms for oil and gas exploration. Inspired by its potential, I have extended the same core algorithmic principles to the field of medical imaging. This approach offers high-resolution imaging at minimal cost, while maintaining safety, making it particularly suitable for remote and underserved areas.

I am deeply honored and grateful to have been recognized by the esteemed IITK Alumni Class of 1965 with the Pioneer and Innovation Award. This recognition is not just an award for me — it is a source of motivation and a spark to translate this innovation into a practical, impactful product that can benefit middle-class communities in India and beyond.

Faculty Awardee · 2025

Tushar Sandhan

Faculty · IIT Kanpur
AI & Computer Vision

It is a great honor to be the first recipient of the Pioneering Research and Innovation Award, IIT Kanpur 2025. In my Perception and Intelligence Lab (PIL), we develop AI and Computer Vision technologies for healthcare, robotics, intelligent sensing, and real-world decision-making systems. My research spans autonomous systems, multimodal learning, and AI-driven solutions for societal challenges.

This award instils the trust in the lab to continue to pursue high-impact research and nurture the next generation of AI innovators among students. I sincerely thank IIT Kanpur for this recognition and express my deep gratitude to the whole batch for their generous support of AI research and education.

Faculty Awardee · 2026

Dr. Raghvendra Kumar Chaudhary

Associate Professor · Electrical Engineering
Antenna Systems & Applied Electromagnetics

My research focuses on developing advanced antenna systems and engineered electromagnetic structures for next-generation unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and aircraft, where reliable wireless communication, low electromagnetic observability, and seamless platform integration are essential.

I sincerely thank the 1965 Batch alumni for their generous support through the Pioneering Research and Innovation Award. This recognition encourages our pursuit of impactful research and innovation in advanced electromagnetic and antenna technologies for next-generation UAV and defence applications.
