Funding diagram — more ways to show it

Same story: ₹2.5 Cr Goal, ₹1 Cr Raised (40%), ₹20 L Utilised (8% of goal). Four fresh directions below.

Option E

Fundraising thermometer

Classic vertical fill — raised level rises toward the goal; utilised shown as a band at the base.

Option F

100-square grid

Each square = ₹2.5 L. 40 filled = raised; the 8 darkest = already utilised; faint = still needed.

Option G

Semicircle gauge

A speedometer-style arc — fills to 40% of goal, with an inner arc for the utilised share.

Option H

Liquid-fill circle

A goal circle filling up like a vessel — the raised level rises; the base band is what's been utilised.
